Sarah Weerstra

Sarah came to HHM in February of 2022 and quickly became part of the team. An eager and bright asset to HHM, Sarah takes a proactive approach to working with clients, other attorneys, and the courts to help clients navigate the legal landscape with confidence and genuineness.

Sarah earned her undergraduate degree in economics with minors in Spanish and philosophy from the University of Wyoming and proceeded to obtain her juris doctor degree from the Alexander Blewett III School of Law in Missoula, Montana. After completing law school, Sarah and her husband relocated to Gillette and have made the community and culture their home.

Sarah is admitted to practice law in Wyoming and is admitted to all Wyoming State Courts.

Outside of the office, Sarah enjoys serving the community through various boards or by coaching youth sports and enjoys spending time in the Big Horns and Black Hills with her husband and dogs whenever she has the chance.
