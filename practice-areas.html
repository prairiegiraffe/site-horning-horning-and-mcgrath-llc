Practice Areas

Business and Commercial

As your general business partner, we provide counsel and structure for your business's ultimate success. HHM is a trusted source knowledgeable in the law and regulations affecting your business and the impact those have on your business as laws change and your business needs evolve. From mergers, acquisitions, and sales to real estate acquisitions and leases to employment agreements and disputes, to contract review, our attorneys are a valuable resource to ensure your business is protected and remains profitable, no matter the changes that come your way. As the economy changes and impacts your business, we'll be there with you through ups and downs rooting for and helping you succeed. HHM can be your outside general counsel for a fraction of the cost of hiring an in-house attorney.

As a small business, HHM understands the toll outside impacts have on your business and we want success for you – that's why we practice practical, efficient, and conscientious representation – to honor you and your business.

Oil and Gas, Mineral Royalties and Energy Law

Oil and gas and/or mineral royalties are often an important piece to your legacy and we want to help you recognize and utilize these assets to their full potential. Let us help you negotiate the best agreement for you and be sure you're not agreeing to terms you may not be comfortable with later on. Helping you understand your rights and negotiate fair returns to protect your legacy, oftentimes your family's legacy, are priorities to our attorneys.

Our attorneys experienced in oil and gas related contracts can help you negotiate, navigate, and realize the full potential of your holdings preserving those interests for your future or attaining the best terms for you and your individual needs.

Real Estate and Land Use

Our attorneys will listen to understand your goals for the transaction, explain any legal implications of the transaction, negotiate the terms most beneficial to you, anticipate and navigate potential setbacks, and see your transaction through closing. Estate Planning

Developing the best estate plan structure for your individual needs is paramount to your ultimate goal when considering an estate plan – making things easier for your loved ones when you pass and avoiding unnecessary and sometimes expensive probate court. We at HHM do not believe in one-size-fits-all estate plans and collaborate with you to customize a plan that fits you and your personal circumstances. There are many factors to consider when creating an estate plan and our attorneys take pride in being thorough and efficient so you can be confident your estate plan will ease the burden of your loss experienced by your loved ones. In most instances we offer flat fee estate planning services so you know exactly how much you will pay. We do not charge you until your plan is completed the way you want it.

Think you've found what you need online with a form on the internet? Does it come with personalized advice and counsel about its effects and validity? Does it fit your needs? An estate plan is a carefully crafted package designed to meet your unique circumstances- what's most important to you? You and your loved ones deserve more than a "one size fits all form" because you, your assets, your family structure, and your priorities are not "one size fits all." What you work hard for should be honored and preserved. HHM's attorneys guide you through the process to tailor an estate plan for you, ensuring your plan is not unnecessarily cumbersome potentially causing complication for your family. We work with you to create a plan that suits your unique needs and simplifies things for your family.

Probate

Wyoming probate administration can be complex and our attorneys are knowledgeable and experienced in all areas of Wyoming probate law, from large estates to small. The loss of a loved one is hard. Probate doesn't have to be the nightmare you've heard about. Allow one of our attorneys to advise and counsel you through the process to navigate and simplify the necessary steps for you while you grieve the loss of a loved one.

At HHM, you'll receive compassionate representation from experienced attorneys who ensure the probate proceedings move as smoothly and as quickly as possible. Every estate and its needs through probate differ – let us be the counsel you can rely on when you're faced with navigating a journey through probate.

Civil Litigation

Whether you are an existing client or have a new dispute, HHM is precisely the team you want on your side when litigation arises. A variety of situations can lead to civil litigation, including disputes between parties when dealing with probate matters, the management of real estate or other property, business relationships, and many others. Although litigation may seem like a daunting task, our attorneys are well-equipped to meet your litigation needs.
