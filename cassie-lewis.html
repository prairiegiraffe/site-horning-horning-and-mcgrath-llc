Cassie Lewis

Cassie, our firm’s administrator and paralegal, has more than 20 years of experience working as a paralegal after graduating from Western Dakota Technical College with her paralegal degree in 2004. She has been with HHM since its inception, providing administrative support to the firm and assisting the attorneys in all practice areas.

Originally from South Dakota, she and her husband have called Gillette home since 2008. Outside of the office, Cassie spends her time supporting her children in their various activities, reading, and gardening.
